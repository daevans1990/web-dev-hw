Bacon and Egg Ramen Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant ramen
  • 3 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon green onions
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Fry bacon and egg to preference
  2. Thinly slice green onions
  3. Prepare ramen noodles instructions on package
  4. Top your prepared ramen with bacon and fried egg. Garnish with green onions and season with salt and pepper
